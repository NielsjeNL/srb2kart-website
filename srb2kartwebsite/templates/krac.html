KRAC Results

KRAC stands for Karthouse Record Attack Competition. Don't do drugs, kids.

Welcome to the results page of the Karthouse Record Attack Competition 2023.
Everything you want to know is right here, in front of your eyes.

To recap: KRAC was a record attack competition, lasting a month.
Every week, two maps would be selected that players would have to record their fastest times for.
Players joined any of their preferred divisions and competed in those.
This page displays the results of the competition.

Results from one division should not be compared to another division.
Each division is subject to different racer statistics. Big Fast is in general faster than Little Guys, for example.
